Experience



Ux Design Humanities Project 2019

In this project, my group and I were given the task of restructuring the information input and output system of the Wilfrid Laurier Humanities Course, as well as modifying the website layout and modernizing it.

Makers Lab Project 2020

In this project, my group and I were given the task of "Hacking the conventional box" and creating something useful for people. We created a Compact Desk Box that stores small tedious items around the person's workspace. We also did this with the use of common and easily attainable materials.

Makers Lab Project 2020
In this project, my group and I were tasked with creating something that revolved around social issues relating to Covid-19 and the pandemic. We decided to create a DIY Projector that projects the screen of a smartphone or Nintendo Switch. We thought this was a good idea because, with unexpected lock-downs, people have to stay home and manage time. This gives them a fun and interesting twist. This item also consists of common, reusable, and easily attainable materials.

Interaction Design Project 2020
In this project, my group and I decided that we would modify the Tinder application for the reason of making the application desirable to use to meet and contact new people. We added features such as a behavior bar with different colours to indicate levels of behavior. We also added a new filter system to help the user filter out individuals they don't wish to interact with.

Makers Lab Project 2021 In this project, my group and I created a tool that would allow individuals with physical disabilities to interact with technological devices much more comfortably and efficiently. We created a device called the "Pair N Play" Pad. This pad would essentially serve as a control centre for these individuals to control their devices. We also integrated specifications that would enable users to connect to (IoT) to enhance smart home experience and usage.
